## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01 | Elevation of Privilege | window:* IPC handlers | low | mitigate | Handlers act only on `BrowserWindow.fromWebContents(event.sender)` — a renderer controls only its own window, never a sibling/main window by id; no payload accepted |
| T-04-02 | Tampering | preload bridge | medium | mitigate | Expose only the three named typed methods (minimizeWindow/toggleMaximizeWindow/closeWindow); never raw ipcRenderer or a generic invoke |
| T-04-03 | Information disclosure | icon path | low | accept | Icon path is a build-time constant under app resources, not renderer-supplied |
</threat_model>
